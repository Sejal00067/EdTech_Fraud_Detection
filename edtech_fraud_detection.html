Model Architecture

FraudShield uses a stacked ensemble of four AI/ML models working in parallel:

🧠 XGBoost Classifier
Tabular feature classification on 47 transaction attributes. Primary decision engine.
📈 LSTM Sequence Model
Detects temporal anomalies across user's historical transaction sequences.
🔍 Isolation Forest
Unsupervised anomaly detection for zero-day fraud patterns not seen in training.
🌐 Graph Neural Network
Maps relationships between users, devices, and payment methods to detect rings.
💡 Final score = weighted average of all models, calibrated via isotonic regression for probability correctness.

EdTech Fraud Detection — Research Summary

Literature Review
AI in digital payments has evolved from rule-based systems (pre-2015) to deep learning ensemble models (2022+), achieving >97% precision on benchmark datasets.
🎓
EdTech Context
Unique fraud vectors: fake enrollments for credential theft, phishing course pages, bulk coupon abuse, identity misuse in scholarship payments.

Regulatory Frame
